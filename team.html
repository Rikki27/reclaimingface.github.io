The Project Team

Please see below for more information on the team responsible for the Reclaiming Face project

Mel

Mel Walker is an undergraduate at Michigan State University studying anthropology and geography. She became interested in digital heritage at the CHI fieldschool at MSU. She currently works at as a survey researcher.

Celeste

Celeste A-Re (digital curator) is a digital assistant at the University of South Carolina and digital archivist at the Library of Congress. Her research interests revolve around transdisciplinary approaches to critical heritage studies and critical race information theory as applied to public history and cultural curation.

Rikki

Rikki Valkema is a researcher and scholar in the field of cultural anthropology specializing in Asian studies. Her interests are specifically centered in South Korean modern media effects on youth and a possible relation between this and an increase in crime

Flora

Flora Feltham is an archivist, librarian, data geek and digital historian from New Zealand. Her research focuses on the intersection of cultural heritage and digital technology, and the print history of Wellington
