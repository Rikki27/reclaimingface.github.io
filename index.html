Reclaiming Face: Mapping Chinese Immigration into the Americas 1785 - 1914

This project aims to increase awareness of an overlooked part of history: Chinese immigration during the long nineteenth century. Reclaiming Face will use an interactive map to show changes in population throughout the Americas over time; reveal the personal narratives of a community; and provide a portal to currently dispersed digital material on this topic.
